PriceThis

Product Specification v1.0

React Native (iOS + Android)

⸻

Vision

PriceThis is the Shazam for expensive things.

Point your camera at anything. Instantly discover what it is, what it’s worth, and why it matters.

The app satisfies one of the most common human curiosities:

“I wonder how much that costs?”

The product should feel magical, effortless and addictive. Every scan should answer that question in under 5 seconds, then immediately pull the user into a deeper rabbit hole of fascinating facts.

Price is the hook.

Curiosity is the product.

⸻

Product Philosophy

Every product decision should satisfy these principles.

* One action before value
* Under 5 seconds to first result
* Every scan teaches something surprising
* Camera-first
* Zero onboarding
* Beautiful enough to screenshot
* AI explains itself
* Every result naturally leads to another question
* Curiosity over information overload

The app should never feel like a shopping app or price comparison tool.

It should feel like an AI-powered curiosity engine for the physical world.

Recognition Results

Never claim certainty.

Preferred wording

This appears to be…

or

This looks like…

Always display confidence.

Example

This appears to be

BMW M340i (2023)

Confidence

96%

Alternative matches

BMW 330i

Audi S4

Never show more than three possibilities.

The Curiosity Engine

Every successful scan must generate at least one genuinely surprising insight.

This is the heart of the product.

Examples

This watch sells above retail.

Only 12,000 of these were ever made.

This house costs more than 99.98% of homes in India.

This car depreciates faster than the average annual salary.

This sneaker originally sold for ₹16,000 and now averages ₹2.4 lakh.

Every scan should leave users saying

“I didn’t know that.”

Curiosity Cards

Below every result are expandable cards.

Cards are generated dynamically based on object type.

Examples

Value History

Ownership Cost

How much does it cost to own annually?

Interesting Facts

Why is this valuable?

Alternatives

What else could I buy for this amount?

Rarity

How many exist?

Market Trends

Is now a good time to buy?

Famous Owners

Who owns this?

Historical Prices

How has the value changed?

Authentication

Common signs of genuine vs fake.

Not every object needs every card.

The AI should choose only the most relevant.

⸻

Personalization

Personalization is optional and always happens after the first value moment.

After the initial result, users may unlock personalized insights by entering:

* Annual income
* Country
* Age (optional)

Once provided, the app generates highly relatable insights.

Examples

This costs 4.8 years of your current income.

You could buy 183 Toyota Corollas instead.

Owning this would consume 62% of your annual income.

This is worth more than your estimated lifetime earnings.

This home costs roughly 340 monthly salaries in your city.

The goal is perspective, not judgment.

The tone must always remain respectful and playful.

Performance Targets

Camera opens under 1 second.

Recognition under 3 seconds.

Results under 5 seconds total.

⸻

Error Handling

If recognition fails

“We’re not confident enough to identify this yet.”

Offer

* Try another angle
* Upload a clearer image
* Search manually

Never fabricate certainty.

⸻

Privacy

Images are processed securely.

Users control scan history.

Delete all data at any time.

Personal financial information remains private.

Personalized comparisons are never shared unless the user explicitly includes them.

⸻

Analytics

Track only meaningful events.

* Camera opened
* Image uploaded
* Scan completed
* Recognition confidence
* Curiosity card opened
* Share initiated
* Share completed
* Personalization unlocked
* Return visit
* Search performed

Avoid tracking unnecessary behavioral data.

North Star Metric

Percentage of completed scans that are shared or followed by another scan within five minutes.

The app succeeds when curiosity compounds. A great scan should naturally lead to either sharing the discovery or scanning the next interesting object.

⸻

Success Metrics

* Camera-to-first-result under 5 seconds
* Scan success rate above 90%
* Share rate above 25%
* Average scans per session above 6
* Day 7 retention above 25%
* App Store rating above 4.8

Final Product Philosophy

PriceThis should never feel like a valuation tool.

It should feel like having the world’s most knowledgeable collector, appraiser and enthusiast in your pocket.

The user never opens PriceThis because they need a price.

They open it because they’re curious.

Every scan should answer one question instantly:

“What’s this worth?”

And immediately spark the next one:

“Wait… really? Tell me more.”

That is the loop that turns a one-time novelty into a habit.
